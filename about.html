About Our School

Learn more about our journey, our mission, and our commitment to education in Malawi.

Our Background

Euthini Secondary School is a government school located in the Northern Region of Malawi, in Mzimba District. It has been serving the community for decades by offering quality education to students from various backgrounds.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to nurture students intellectually, morally, and socially to become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Core Values

  • 🤝 Integrity & Respect
  • 🌍 Community Engagement
